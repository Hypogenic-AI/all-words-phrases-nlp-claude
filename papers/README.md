# Downloaded Papers

## Core Papers (Relevance 3)

1. **Token Erasure as a Footprint of Implicit Vocabulary Items in LLMs** (2406.20086_token_erasure_implicit_vocabulary.pdf)
   - Authors: Feucht, Atkinson, Wallace, Bau (2024)
   - arXiv: 2406.20086
   - Why relevant: **Foundational paper** — proposes erasure score method to read out LLM implicit vocabulary

2. **Are Representations Built from the Ground Up?** (2210.03575_representations_ground_up.pdf)
   - Authors: Liu, Neubig (2022)
   - arXiv: 2210.03575
   - Why relevant: Studies local compositionality in LMs; finds LMs don't align with human compositionality judgments

3. **Idiom-Aware Compositional Distributed Semantics** (not downloaded — 2017 paper, pre-transformer)
   - Why relevant: Early work on incorporating idiom awareness into compositional models

## Highly Relevant Papers (Relevance 2)

4. **Neurons in Large Language Models: Dead, N-gram, Positional** (2309.04827_neurons_dead_ngram_positional.pdf)
   - Authors: Voita et al. (2023)
   - arXiv: 2309.04827
   - Why relevant: Identifies n-gram neurons — complementary evidence for multi-token pattern processing

5. **Probing for Idiomaticity in Vector Space Models** (eacl2021_probing_idiomaticity.pdf)
   - Authors: Garcia et al. (2021)
   - Venue: EACL 2021
   - Why relevant: Establishes probing methodology for non-compositional expressions

6. **Semantics of Multiword Expressions in Transformer-Based Models: A Survey** (2401.15393_mwe_transformer_survey.pdf)
   - Year: 2024
   - arXiv: 2401.15393
   - Why relevant: Comprehensive survey of MWE handling in transformers

7. **CLCL: Non-compositional Expression Detection** (acl2023_clcl_noncompositional.pdf)
   - Year: 2023
   - Venue: ACL 2023
   - Why relevant: Contrastive learning approach for detecting non-compositional expressions

8. **Interpreting Token Compositionality in LLMs: A Robustness Analysis** (2410.12924_token_compositionality_robustness.pdf)
   - Year: 2024
   - arXiv: 2410.12924
   - Why relevant: Directly tests robustness of token composition in LLMs

9. **Using Shapley Interactions to Understand How Models Use Structure** (2403.13106_shapley_interactions_structure.pdf)
   - Authors: Singhvi et al. (2024)
   - arXiv: 2403.13106
   - Why relevant: Alternative method (Shapley interactions) for detecting non-compositional semantics
